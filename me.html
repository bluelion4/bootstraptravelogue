This portal is about mixing my hobbies of Riding and Travel Photography . Since Childhood it was my dream to own a DSLR camera and capture moments on a large scale. Finally after few years of getting into a job , I managed to buy my first DSLR, Nikon D90. I already owned a Pulsar 150 V2 , but to reach the remote places of Maharashtra I needed a bike which could do off-roading as well as cruise . Ktm Duke 200 was my choice and It has exceeded my expectations.
